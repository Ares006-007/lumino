Our Story

Built on a simple belief:
technology should feel as good as it performs.

Lumino was founded in 2024 by a team of engineers and designers who were frustrated with a market that forced a choice — either sacrifice design for performance, or performance for aesthetics.

We decided not to choose. Every product in our catalogue has been selected against a strict set of criteria: build quality, performance benchmarks, repairability, and design restraint. If a product doesn't belong, it doesn't ship.

01

Curated with intention

We evaluate hundreds of products before adding anything to our catalogue. Our team tests each device against real-world use cases — not spec sheets. Only the best 1% makes the cut.

02

Designed to last

We favour aluminium over plastic, sapphire over glass, and software that gets better over time. Products we sell today should still be relevant three years from now.

03

Honest pricing

Premium doesn't have to mean overpriced. We work directly with manufacturers to offer fair margins — you pay for quality, not for marketing spend.

04

Support that cares

Every Lumino customer gets a dedicated support specialist — not a chatbot. We answer questions before, during, and long after your purchase.
